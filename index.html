The "techtonic" shift in the democratic process is here! Welcome to Citizense: your guide to civic participation, scientific understanding, and government education. Let's start the government transformance today!

Providing you deep anaylsis and digital participation on legislative pieces of writing. We are here to make sure you get more than information, but also an analaysis on potential pieces that are law to your democracy.

Goals

Transparency

To create trust and empathy amongst one another, we aim to provide as an unbiased resource with nonpartisan motives that will encourage others to be informed and educated on community decisions.

Engagement

Beyond learning and being informed is having an active voice in the process, not just immediately before the election season, but also throughout daily lives on all levels: local, state, and national.

Accountability

Citizens and politicians need to bridge their concerns to express one cohesive goal to reduce upsets and wrongdoings in the future
